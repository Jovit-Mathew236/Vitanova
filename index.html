Experience Vitanova:
Embrace New Life in Christ!

What?

Vitanova is a program set up by the Jesus Youth seniors in our college for you to spend three unforgettable days with Jesus.

Why?

You might or might not have attended many programs here in the college. Rest assured, Vitanova is nothing like you'd ever expect. The vibe is something you've never encountered. Experiencing it is the only way out of your ambiguity as well into witnessing the beauty of the program.

Programs?

Fun activities, games, songful treat by the music band, yum-yum food and stay are the highlights.

Funding?

Vitanova is funded by the Jesus Youth passouts of the college with the aim of ensuring that you experience what they had and not miss it.
